HOW IT
WORKS

Steps to getting yourself a new friend:


Look through the site and find an animal you like! Make sure to choose the best pet for your type of living.

Book a visit 7 days in advance! We will ensure that no one takes the chosen animal before your visit!

We're excited to meet you! You'll learn a lot about the animals in our shelter and we'll help you make the right decision!

Working time:

MON-FRI: 08:00-19:00

SAT-SUN: 08:00-15:00

Animal Shelter Binky is a non-profit organization* settled in Split that is commited to saving animals and giving them a new home. Animal Shelter Binky currently consists of 12 hardworking volunteers.

The brains behind the project are Ammar Ibrahimović and Marin Boban who are happy that the project turned out so well.

We want to thank you for your support and donations. This wouldn't be possible without each and every one of you!

*the organization is purely fictional
